V1 builds on the MVP with social login, in-app messaging, improved item discovery, and email notifications.

## Social Login

- Google sign-in
- Apple sign-in
- Existing email/password auth remains available

## In-App Chat

- Users can exchange messages on the platform (send and reply)
- Chat is tied to a specific item
- Chat view layout:
  - Item thumbnail at the top
  - Conversation messages at the bottom
  - Clicking the thumbnail navigates to the item detail page
- Chat list page showing all active conversations

## Item Browsing

- Dedicated items page showing all available items (not only via user profiles)
- Text search by item title
- Basic filter options:
  - Type: accessory, clothing, other
  - Monetization: free, lending, other
  - Child age

## Notifications

- Email notifications for reservation events:
  - Seller: "Someone reserved your item"
  - Buyer: "Your reservation is about to expire"
  - Seller: "A reservation on your item expired"
